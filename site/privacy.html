Privacy Policy

How we handle the information you share through this website.

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

AragoCor Minerals LLC ("AragoCor", "we", "us") provides this website to share information about our high-purity natural oolitic aragonite and to let technical and commercial visitors request samples, documentation and pricing. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

We collect only the information you choose to provide, primarily through our inquiry and sample-request forms. This may include:

  • Your name, company, work email and (optionally) phone number;
  • Details about your intended application, requested grade, particle size, estimated volume, delivery location and timeline;
  • Any message or specification you send us.

Standard web-server logs may record basic technical data such as browser type and pages visited. We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information.

How we use it

  • To respond to your sample, technical-data, pricing or consultation request;
  • To prepare quotations and supporting documentation relevant to your application;
  • To maintain a record of business correspondence.

Sharing

We do not sell your information. We do not share the specifications or application details you provide with third parties, except where necessary to fulfil your request (for example, arranging shipment of a sample) or where required by law.

Data retention

We retain inquiry information for as long as needed to serve your request and maintain ordinary business records. You may ask us to delete your information at any time.

Your choices

To request access to, correction of, or deletion of the information you have provided, contact us at info@aragocorminerals.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to info@aragocorminerals.com.
