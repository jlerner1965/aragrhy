Glossary

A precise vocabulary

Aragonite
An orthorhombic polymorph of calcium carbonate (CaCO₃). The mineral form AragoCor supplies.
Calcite
The most common, trigonal polymorph of CaCO₃. The dominant mineral in conventional limestone.
Oolitic
Composed of small, concentrically layered spheres formed by mineral precipitation around microscopic nuclei.
OMRI
Organic Materials Review Institute — certification body for inputs approved for organic agriculture.
LCA
Life Cycle Analysis — formal accounting of environmental impacts across a product's lifecycle.
XRD
X-ray Diffraction — technique used to identify mineral phases.
EDS
Energy-Dispersive Spectroscopy — technique used for elemental analysis.
